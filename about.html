Who Am I?

Bruce An

When the first iPhone came out in 2007, I thought it was a game changer. Apple just enabled everyone in the world the power of a pocketable, touch screen computer phone. It reinvented the cellphone and changed the way we lived. Today, there are new technologies under development that I believe will change the way we live once again (AI, AR, Blockchain). The computing power is there, but how do we interact with these new things? I think growth and innovation in technology will come from software that can use these technologies while balancing form and function. It needs to be logical yet human beacuse it's the user/customer experience that causes people to buy products from the same company over and over again. The challenges of tomorrow will require innovative solutions, and I think the best solutions combine creativity and logic.
